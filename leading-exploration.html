What

What You'll Learn

A self-paced, practical curriculum designed to help aspiring leaders:

  • Understand the realities of leadership
  • Evaluate their strengths and development areas
  • Explore topics like Crucial Conversations, Getting Things Done, and Situational Leadership
  • Complete a Leader Readiness Assessment and a Self-Discovery Worksheet
  • Engage in coaching conversations with their direct leader
  • Focus on self-assessment, skill development, goal setting, and continuous learning
Who

Who Should Participate

Target Audience:

  • Individual contributors in Operations who are interested in a leadership role and want to proactively prepare for the opportunity
  • Employees seeking to develop leadership skills before taking on formal leadership responsibilities
  • High-potential team members identified for future leadership positions
How

How It Works

Learning Format:

  • Self-paced e-Learning platform
  • Includes videos, assessments, hands-on activities, and coaching with the learner's direct leader
  • Mixed media format called the Self-Discovery Kit
  • Interactive modules and practical exercises
  • Progress tracking and completion certificates
When

When & Duration

Time Commitment:

  • Total time: Approximately 4 hours
  • Consumed at the learner's own pace
  • Available 24/7 through the LMS platform
  • Can be completed over days, weeks, or months
  • No set deadlines - fully flexible scheduling
